Bethesda Twitch Integration

by Flenarn

The Bethesda Twitch Integration (BTI) system allows for Twitch Integration with the different Bethesda games!

Let your viewers interact with your game, live, in ways never before possible!

Will they unleash chaos in your game, drown you in fantastic loot, just play God and change the weather at their will, or maybe throw a deathclaw at you? Who knows really, but now it’s possible!

Source

  • #points - See how many points you have
  • #give - Syntax: #give stimpak 2 - Purchase an item, ammo amounts are x10
  • #spawn - Syntax: #spawn ghoul - Spawns a mob, or group of mobs
  • #weather - Syntax: #weather clearday - Changes the weather
  • #misc - Syntax: #misc heal - Triggers a custom event
  • #randomspawn - Spawns a random enemy from the spawn list. Costs 1000 points
  • #givepoints - Syntax: #givepoints @username 100 - give another viewer your points
Points can be gained by watching, purchasing subscriptions, or spending bits. Spending real money is completely optional, and totally not required.
  • Watching - 2 points per minute
  • Buying, or gifting a Tier 1 Sub - 500 points
  • Buying, or gifting a Tier 2 Sub - 1000 points
  • Buying, or gifting a Tier 3 Sub - 1500 points
  • Spending Bits - 10 points per bit
